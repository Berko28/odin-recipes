World's best lasagna!

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

The ingredients list:

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

There are two parts of the instructions, one will be of how to prepare the ingredients for the dish while the other is about layering the lasagna.

Preparing the ingredients

This will be the brief overview of what you should do with the ingredients listed above:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions (check the instructions below)
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Layering the lasagna

This will be the order you should follow to properly layer the lasagna:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzeralla slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan
